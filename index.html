Zachary T. Harmany, PhD

I'm a project scientist in the Department of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine at UC Davis Medical Center where I work with Prof. Richard Levenson.
Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Wisconsin-Madison where I worked with Prof. Robert Nowak.
I received my degree from Duke University in Electrical and Computer Engineering. My PhD advisor was Prof. Rebecca Willett. I also worked closely with Prof. Roummel Marcia when I was a visiting researcher in Applied Math at The University of California, Merced.


More about me can be found in my bio and my CV.
